Net Low
What is Net-Low?

Net-Low is a groundbreaking employee engagement platform developed by The Green Solve, designed to help businesses track, measure, and verify their carbon offsets through everyday sustainability actions. With the increasing demand for transparency in sustainability reporting and the need to liminate greenwashing, Net Low provides a data-driven approach to achieve measurable environmental impact.

How Net-Low Works
Net-Low integrates gamification and real-time tracking to empower employees to participate in sustainability tasks that directly contribute to their company’s ESG Environmental, Social, and Governance) goals. It helps businesses achieve Net-Zero emissions by making sustainability simple, actionable, and verifiable.
Employees can log activities such as:
  • Walking or biking to work
  • Recycling initiatives
  • Reducing energy usage at home or work
  • Using eco-friendly products
These actions are tracked and converted into verified carbon offsets that are aggregated at the company level, ensuring transparent reporting for ESG compliance.

Why Net-Low is Unique

Unlike traditional sustainability tools, Net-Low focuses on both individual actions and corporate sustainability goals, bridging the gap between employee engagement and verified environmental impact. It’s not just a platform—it’s a movement to make sustainability transparent, measurable, and accessible to all.
