Welcome to the Carbon
Footprint Calculator!

This comprehensive tool helps you measure, track, and manage
greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions across your organization.

Scope-1

Includes direct emissions from owned or controlled sources such as fuel combustion and manufacturing processes.

Scope-2

Covers indirect emissions from the generation of purchased electricity, steam, heating, and cooling.

Scope-3

Expands the assessment to all other indirect emissions across the value chain, such as raw material production, transportation, and waste disposal.

If you’re here, you likely value a greener, smarter, and more profitable future for your business. We help you transform sustainability into a strategic advantage while making a positive impact.
